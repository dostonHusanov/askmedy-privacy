Privacy Policy for Ask Medy

Last updated: November 2025

Ask Medy is a health‑assistant mobile app designed to help users understand common health issues, manage pill reminders, and access AI‑powered guidance. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

No personal data is shared with third parties. The app stores limited user data only on your device or securely in Firebase (if enabled).

a. User‑Provided Information

  • Name, surname, age
  • Phone number (stored locally in the app only)
  • Pill reminders you create

b. Automatically Collected Information

Ask Medy does not collect analytics, location, or background data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule pill reminders
  • To display personalized health information
  • To power AI chat responses

3. AI Chat (Gemini API)

Your AI chat queries are sent securely to Google Gemini API to generate answers. Ask Medy does not store or log your conversations.

4. Data Security

All user data is stored securely using recommended Android practices. Sensitive data is not transmitted to any third party except Gemini API for chat responses.

5. Children's Privacy

Ask Medy is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. You will be notified inside the app when updates occur.
